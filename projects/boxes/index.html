Music Boxes | 2016
Previous Next

I designed these instruments for the class "Circuit breakers!" where I made instruments from hacked electric circuitry. This instrument uses a modified design of Nicolas Collins’ “Love Parade”. This circuitry works with the CD4017 counter/divider, hacking it to work as a sequencer. It originally controls LEDs, however when hooked up to a VCO (Voltage-controlled oscillator) then the circuitry starts functioning as a wavetable synthesizer. My design separates the crucial parts, therefore there’s a box for

  • The tempo
  • The waves
  • And the LEDs.
