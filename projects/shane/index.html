Olympic Dark Horse | 2019
Olympics 2020? Say what?

I edited the episodes of Olympic Dark Horse as a freelancing editor for Shane's YouTube channel with 4000 subscribers, In a collaboration with Arrabona Studio . I was hired to do Episode 15, where I worked closely with the director Ádám Miklósi to develop the concept. The Olympic Dark Horse series is intended to continue until the Tokio Olympics 2020. The project fell through, but the episode came out dope! Best of luck to the athletes!
