Superhero | 2018

In a team of 3 I assumed the role Game Designer and collaborated with the Nepali educational organization Karkhana to create a game that promotes scientific thinking to children in middle school. Our goal was to immerse the children in the stories of aspiring Nepalis and create role models from the local tech scene. I made this while taking the course Making Education through the Interactive Media department at NYU Abu Dhabi.
We met and interviewed all the subjects of the game to be able to add a personal essence to the game.

Shivani

She is the founder of the company Junkeri, that manufactures jewellery enhanced with technology.
Special skill: she can make special wearables that make the player stronger.
