Puppet Gene | 2018
Previous Next

I was assistant editor on director Waleed Talal Al Madani's documentary short, Puppet Gene. The documentary short follows a week in the life of Lili Pilári, who is the youngest member of the renown Pilári family. She is preparing for her thesis presentation with her father. I learned a lot in the process of making this film, and from Waleed who is an experienced freelancer in videography and editing.
