The Gazelle | 2018

In 2018 Spring I was the Video Desk Editor of The Gazelle, a UAE newspaper.
I produced short videos about issues concerning the student life of NYUAD. Managed the production team and created a shooting schedule. I worked as an all around videographer on the productions, shooting and recording sound, as well as editing the videos. Recruited participants (10+) and conceptualized content that reaches the student body of 1200+.

Cats on the NYUAD Campus
Role: Director, Interviewer, Editor
This video was based on the frustration that started around the presence of cats on the NYUAD campus.

The student body is still divided how to react to the cats that started dwelling the university grounds around the end of 2017. Many cat lovers felt like the university had an obligation to take care of these animals, as their wellbeing affects health and the environment of the campus. This video covered just that.

Al Bloom Interview
Role: Director, Interviewer, Editor
An interview with the former Vice Chancellor, Al Bloom.

Al Bloom, VC of NYUAD for 8 years stepped down in 2019. For this occasion our team decided to interview him, to let him talk about the time he spent establishing the world class institution.
